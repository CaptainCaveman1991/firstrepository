Jarrasic Park

Whats it all about?

Jarrasic park is an American science fiction media franchise created by Michael Crichton and centered on a disastrous attempt to create a theme park of cloned dinosaurs.

Jarrasic Park Film

Jarrasic Park was turned into a film in 1993 by Steven Spielberg, It is the first installment in the Jurassic Park franchise, and the first film in the original Jurassic Park trilogy, and is based on Michael Crichton's 1990 novel of the same name, with a screenplay by Crichton and David Koepp.
